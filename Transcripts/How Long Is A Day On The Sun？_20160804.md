## Key Concepts

**Day**  
A unit of time equal to 86,400 seconds or the time it takes for the Earth to rotate once on its axis

**Rotation Period**  
The time it takes for an object to complete one rotation on its axis

**Gravitational Time Dilation**  
The phenomenon where time passes slower near a massive object due to its strong gravitational field
