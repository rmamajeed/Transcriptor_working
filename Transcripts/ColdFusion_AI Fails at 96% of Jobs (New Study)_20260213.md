## Key Concepts

**Artificial Intelligence (AI)**  
AI refers to the development of computer systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making.

**Remote Labor Index (RLI)**  
RLI is a method of evaluating AI's performance by comparing it to human workers on real-world tasks.

*Both measure labor market efficiency*

The RLI and Labor Market Index share a structural similarity in that they both quantify the efficiency and productivity of labor. For instance, the RLI can be used to analyze the effects of AI on labor market outcomes, such as employment rates and wage growth.

*Both involve adaptive optimization*

The principle of adaptive optimization, which is fundamental to AI, has a direct analogue in evolutionary adaptation in biology.
