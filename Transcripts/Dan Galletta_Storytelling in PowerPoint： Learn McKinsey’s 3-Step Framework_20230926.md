## Key Concepts

**SCR framework**  
A storytelling framework used by McKinsey, consisting of Situation, Complication, and Resolution

**Dot-dash structure**  
A framework for writing storylines, using dots for key statements and dashes for supporting data

The dot-dash structure, with its mix of tightly and loosely connected elements, parallels the modular design approach in engineering, where systems are built from interchangeable, autonomous modules.
