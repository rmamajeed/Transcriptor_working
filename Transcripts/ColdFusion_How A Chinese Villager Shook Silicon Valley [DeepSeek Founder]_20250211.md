**Deep Learning**

**Artificial General Intelligence**  
A type of artificial intelligence that can perform any intellectual task that a human can

**Open Source**  
A type of software development where the source code is made available for others to use and modify

**Machine Learning**  
A subset of artificial intelligence that involves training machines to learn from data

*Similarities in neural network structures*

Both artificial neural networks and biological neural networks rely on complex interconnected systems to process and transmit information. This connection has significant implications for the development of more efficient and adaptive AI systems.

Understanding how economic systems reach equilibrium can provide insights into how AGI can be designed to optimize its performance across various tasks. This connection highlights the potential for interdisciplinary approaches to solving complex problems in AI.
