Discussion Topics

- Here are 4 key ideas or concepts discussed in the transcription: 
- A2A Protocol (Agent-to-Agent Protocol): ** A new Google protocol designed to standardize communication and interoperability between AI agents developed using different frameworks and by various companies.
- Agent Card: ** A core concept within A2A that acts like a "business card" for an AI agent, detailing its name, description, expected inputs/outputs, available skills, and example queries.
- Standardized Communication (Messages & Tasks): ** A2A standardizes how agents send information to each other using HTTP/HTTPS, primarily through "messages" for immediate responses and "tasks" for long-running operations that provide status updates and artifacts.
- Agent Executor: ** A component in A2A that wraps an underlying AI agent, providing standardized `execute` and `cancel` functions to trigger the agent's work and manage its lifecycle.
